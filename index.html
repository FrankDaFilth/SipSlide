The Functionality Consumers Want , The Sustainability the Planet Needs.

SipSlide can lids are easy-open, re-closable, liquid-tight and spill-proof. They're perfect for on-the-go Millennials and Zoomers, who want convenience and will pay a premium for it. SipSlide is great for outdoor adventures, commutes, school lunches, concerts, the office, and bars/restaurants! With only 9% of all plastic packaging being recycled (compared to 50% of aluminum), consumers are demanding aluminum packaging as they seek more sustainable solutions. SipSlide is bringing functional, sustainable packaging to consumers without the use of planet-harming plastic.

Simple and Intuitive

SipSlide can lids are as easy to use as standard "pull-tab" lids. Simply lift the handle to break the seal, slide open to drink the beverage, and slide back to re-close (then open/re-close over and over again!).

SipSlide reclosable lids are designed for scale. Join the Circular Economy with SipSlide

What is the Circular Economy? Why does it Matter?

Simply put, the circular economy is the future of all consumer goods. The time is now to transition from a Linear Economy (starting with inputs and ending with waste) to a Circular Economy where recycled and reclaimed materials are the new inputs.

Why Use SipSlide?

Only 9% of plastic packaging is recycled, while over 50% of aluminum is reused. SipSlide allows brands to “close the loop” by providing a 100% recyclable solution with the reclosability of plastic. Our one-of-a-kind innovation is built with the planet in mind. See more about our sustainability and recyclability initiatives here.
